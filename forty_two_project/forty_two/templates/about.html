About This is not your typical question and answer site.

Here, the priority is leaving answers instead of posting questions. There are times when we encounter problems in the wild and manage to solve them, either by luck, skill or both. This is for the times when we stumble across such useful solutions that we may not know the questions to. Instead of discarding a discovered answer, post it here: perhaps then some person will find it in a query.
